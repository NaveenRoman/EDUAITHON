Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Our vision is to transform engineering education globally through innovative use of Artificial Intelligence, Sustainable Practices, and Global Collaboration. We aim to create a world where educators and students are empowered to excel in a rapidly evolving AI-driven environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster innovation in engineering education by providing a platform for knowledge sharing, AI-driven learning, and global collaboration. Through ICTIEE 2026, we support educators, researchers, and students in creating impactful solutions that advance education and empower the next generation of engineers.
